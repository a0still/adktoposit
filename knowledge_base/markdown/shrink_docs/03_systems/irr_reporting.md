## Inventory Recap Report (IRR) & Drilldown

### What is the IRR?
* Monthly reporting that tracks key metrics at **store and department level**.
* Key metrics tracked include:
    * Inventory levels
    * Book vs. Sku
    * Purchases
    * Markup/Markdowns
    * Sales
* Reporting tracks each metric and historical reporting is available for previous months.

### What is the IRR Drilldown?
* A **Power BI tool** that directs you toward the issues that may be impacting your store.
* Allows you to identify issues at more of a **transactional level**.

### Difference between IRR Tool and IRR Drilldown?
* Present the same information visually differently.
* **IRR Tool:** Updated monthly, shows 24 rolling months of data in graph form.
* **IRR Drilldown:** Shows up to 36 rolling months of data in graph form, AND provides a detailed view of transactions impacting a store in the most recent month.

### What to Look for When Researching IRR?
* Review departmental information for areas of concern.
* **Primary alerts for indicating shrink growth:**
    * Month over Month Book Inventory Growth.
    * Month over Month Book vs. SKU growth.
* Review same month IRR alerts and item alerts.
* Identify drivers of red flags:
    * On-hand Changes (OH Changes, Stolen, ETC)
    * Markdowns
    * Purchases
    * Sales
