## Book vs. SKU Deep Dive

### Understanding Impacts on Book vs SKU

#### Things that impact BOTH Book AND SKU:
* MTR - Merchandise (i.e.: between stores)
* Warehouse claims
* Merchandise sales
* Merchandise refunds
* RCTR/Dot.com claims
* Supplier claims
* Virtual claims
* Store Use
* Markdowns
* Markups
* Donations
* In Transit
* Finalized warehouse invoices

#### Things that impact SKU ONLY:
* On-hand changes
* Scanned out stolen
* Inventory increases
* Inventory decreases
* Open receivings (finalized)
* *Dollar value of open receivings will impact Book only on inventory day*

#### Things that impact ONLY Book:
* HO (Home Office) journal entries
* Retail correction
* Sales adjustments
* MTR - Financial (i.e.: auto MTRs)
* EDI - Releasing for payment

* Claim chargebacks
* Credit memos
* CFT payments (i.e.: alcohol)
* Pay from scan accruals
* After inventory adjustment (for the month after inventory only)
* Pre-charged invoices
* 119x entries
* Unfinalized invoices
