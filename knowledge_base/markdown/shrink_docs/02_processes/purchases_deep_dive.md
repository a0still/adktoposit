## Deep Diving Purchases

Purchases may be driving increases in ending inventory levels, especially if they are unnecessary.

### Purchase Components Research Focus:

* **Components highlighted in RED** require the **most research** but tend to be where you find the **most exceptions**:
    * Invoice payments (ex. pay from receipt (PFR) receivings)
    * Retail corrections
    * Warehouse trucks

* **Components highlighted in GREEN** are the **easiest to research** but tend to produce **fewer exceptions**:
    * MTRs (merchandise transfer requests)
    * Property loss claims – Through WCS (Walmart Claims Services)
    * Return Center claims
    * Shrink result (month after inventory only)

* **All other components of purchases:**
    * CFT Alcohol Payments (where applicable)
    * Claim chargebacks
    * Claims filed (ex. proof of deliveries (PODs), WMDC Return Request Claims)
    * Credit memos
    * Journal entries (credits or debits)
    * Merchandise transmittals
    * Optical labs – Walmart Vision Centers only
    * Pay from scan transactions
    * State charges/fees (ex. CRV charges) – where applicable
    * Warehouse credits
